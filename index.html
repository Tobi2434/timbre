Welcome to the Sound Questionnaire

Thank you for participating in this questionnaire! Before you begin, please ensure that your sound volume is adjusted to 20% for the best experience.

This questionnaire will guide you through a series of sounds and ask for your feedback to help us improve sound design and interaction. Make sure you're in a quiet environment for optimal results.

Instructions:

  • Adjust your device volume to 20%.
  • Use headphones for the best audio quality.
  • Complete the questionnaire only once.
  • You can compare by playing a single note, or you can press multiple notes or chords simultaneously.

Tips

    It is recommended to repeatedly listen to different timbres for the same word pair to compare and rank them before scoring, rather than listening to one timbre at a time, as that may cause you to hesitate for a long time.
